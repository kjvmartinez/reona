What is EcoLab?

EcoLab is a research guild where faculty and students collaborate on biodiversity and conservation research in Palawan.

  • Wildlife Conservation
  • Biodiversity Assessment
  • Ecosystem Restoration
  • Human–Environment Interactions

What Makes EcoLab Different?

  • Guild-based learning
  • Hands-on fieldwork
  • Real-world impact
  • Collaborative research
