About

Environmental scientist, academic leader, and researcher dedicated to sustainable development, biodiversity conservation, and science-based policy in Palawan and across the Philippines.

Associate Professor III & Dean, College of Sciences

A seasoned environmental scientist and academic administrator with doctoral training from the University of the Philippines Los Baños, and international graduate studies in Sweden and Japan. Dr. Ona leads research, policy, and academic programs at Palawan State University with a focus on sustainability, public health, and biodiversity.

Dr. Ona has served as University Research Director and Intellectual Property Office Director at PSU, and currently sits as Permanent Alternate Member of the Tubbataha Protected Area Management Board. He has led internationally funded research projects on neglected tropical diseases and Palawan's biodiversity, working alongside institutions such as the London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine and the University of the Philippines Los Baños.
